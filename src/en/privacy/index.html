Legal

Privacy policy.

Compliant with Quebec's Act respecting the protection of personal information in the private sector (Law 25). Last updated: April 18, 2026.

What we collect

When you fill out the contact form, we collect: your name, your email, the piece you are interested in (if you indicate one), and the content of your message. No other personal information is collected automatically on this site — no advertising cookies, no third-party tracking.

Why

This information is used solely to respond to you and to route your inquiry to the atelier. We do not use it for other purposes, do not sell it, and do not share it with any commercial third party.

How long

Form submissions are retained for a maximum of twelve (12) months from the date of receipt, after which they are automatically deleted. If a commercial relationship is established, the information required for that relationship is kept in accordance with applicable legal obligations.

Where

Submissions are routed to the atelier's email inbox, hosted on a service compliant with the GDPR and Quebec Law 25. The site itself is served by Cloudflare Pages (global network infrastructure with SOC 2 and ISO 27001 compliance). Analytics processing, if enabled, uses Plausible — self-hosted or EU-based, cookieless.

Your rights

Under Law 25, you have the right to access your information, to have it corrected, to withdraw your consent, and to request its deletion. To exercise these rights, write to privacy@emerald.co. We respond within thirty (30) days.

Officer

Emerald's personal information protection officer can be reached at privacy@emerald.co.

Important note

This site is currently a portfolio demonstration by Álvaro Ramírez Núñez (ramirez.dev). Form submissions are processed for demonstration purposes only and do not trigger any real commercial operation. No personal information is retained beyond the demo session until a real production is deployed.
