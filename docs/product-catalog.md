## Image sourcing plan (demo)

- **Priority A:** find 3-5 high-quality emerald macro shots that are royalty-free and exclusive-feeling (Pexels curated / Unsplash+ paid / Adobe Stock). Avoid anything in the first page of Google Images.
- **Priority B:** for product mockups, either (a) license real jewelry photography from a stock vendor that shows emerald rings/necklaces in editorial style, or (b) commission one carefully styled render from a freelance 3D artist ($100-300 per piece).
- **Hero:** single WebGL emerald (one model, Draco-compressed, Poly Haven HDRI). See webgl-hero-jewelry.js.

## Do not do

- Use obviously fake AI-generated jewelry with warped hands, extra fingers, nonsense prongs.
- Reuse the same 3 stock images across different "pieces." Luxury buyers notice.
- Quote prices that telegraph "demo" — e.g. `$1.00` or `$99,999,999`.
- Name pieces "Emerald Ring 01."
